Travel Information

  • - Currency: U.S. Dollar (USD)
  • - Language: English and Indigenous Tanitian
  • - There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees
  • - The drinking age on Taniti is 18. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between 12:00am - 9:00 a.m.
  • - Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States)
  • - Many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly

Latest News

  • - New Airport Terminal: Taniti is expanding its airport to accommodate larger jets by 2025.
  • - Golf Course Update: The new 9-hole golf course will be ready next year.
  • - Festival Announcement: The annual Tanitian Cultural Festival will be held in July.
